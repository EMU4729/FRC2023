# Robot Port Layout

## CAN

- 1: Drive Left Master
- 2: Drive Left Slave
- 3: Drive Right Master
- 4: Drive Right Slave

- 5: Upper Arm Master
- 6: Upper Arm Slave

- 7: Fore Arm Master
- 8: Fore Arm Slave

- 9: Subarm Pivot

## PWM (0-9)

- 0: Subarm Rotation Servo
- 1: Gripper Servo

# DIO (0-26)

- 0, 1: Subarm Pivot Encoder
- 2, 3: Subarm Rotation Encoder

- 4, 5: Drive Left Encoder
- 6, 7: Drive Right Encoder

- 8, 9: Fore Arm Encoder
- 10 11: Upper Arm Encoder
